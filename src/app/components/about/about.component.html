Classic and Authentic

Its produces thick and silky yogurt as the clay allows the water to evaporate slowly through the micro pores. Beside, it contains several minerals which adds flavour and nutrition to the yogurt. However,clay is an alkaline substance that can balance out the acidity of the yogurt to some extent. Its small size allows the making of small batches, to have fresh yogurt on hand.
